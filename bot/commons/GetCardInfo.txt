```ansi
!c command works such that it queries Scryfall for a card.

Use as close to the correct name as possible! and it will work!

The preset reactions have various commands:

Books: I will read you the rules of the card
Date: I will read you the sets this card has been printed in
Dollars: I will read you the price of the card according to Scryfall

Example:

!c Avacyn, Angel of Hope
```
